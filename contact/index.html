Alumni

We have 3,000 reasons to love Chicago

There are more than 3,000 people in the Chicago area who got their start at Syracuse University. Armed with their degrees and shaped by their experiences on and off the SU campus, our alumni are making a difference, finding success, and supporting SU in Chicago and beyond.

Take a look at some of our Chicago stars, and read more about how the SU Alumni Association can help you stay connected.

Join the SU Alumni Club of Chicago

If you’re looking for Orange in the Windy City, you can find it!

The SU Alumni Club of Chicago has hundreds of active members and hosts dozens of events throughout the year. SU alumni clubs around the world draw thousands of grads together to enjoy a wide variety of social and educational programming. Events range from faculty speakers to career networking opportunities, to game-watching events and evenings at the theater.
