About Us

The Office of Government and Community Relations advances the University's interests at all levels of federal, state and local government. 

The office supports the work, scholarly research and activities of the University, and seeks to connect the University’s initiatives and priorities with government and community resources; and in turn, provides counsel and guidance on policies, legislation and issues impacting the University.

The Office is a liaison with neighborhood, community, and business organizations on matters pertaining to campus development, and seeks to create mutually beneficial relationships locally as an engaged civic, cultural, and business partner.
